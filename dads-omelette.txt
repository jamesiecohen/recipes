crack 5 eggs into mixing bowl
add 2 tablespoons cream
whisk with fork

add tablespoon butter to teflon pan
medium heat until melted
swirl to reduce foam
pour in egg mixture
let sit for 30 seconds
push in the semi-solid sides with a spatula
liquid egg will spill onto empty parts of pan, let that set for 30 seconds
repeat until set
fold over and plate
add s+p
